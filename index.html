01. Site Pe Entry: The Basics

IS 2502 Kya Hai?

Bhai, concrete akela compression (dabao) jhel sakta hai, par tension (khichav) aate hi toot jata hai. Tension lene ke liye hum usme Sariya (Steel/Rebar) daalte hain.

Ab site pe sariya seedha thodi lagta hai! Uske Hooks banate hain, Stirrups (Ring) banate hain. Agar galat angle ya galat machine se mod diya, toh steel ke andar Micro-cracks aa jayenge aur load aane pe fail ho jayega. IS 2502 yahi rules deta hai ki bina damage kiye steel ko kaise modna hai.

02. Mandrel Radius: The Bending Rule

Never Bend Sharp! 🚫

Sariya kabhi bhi 90 degree sharp edge pe nahi modte. Ek gol pin (jise Mandrel kehte hain) ke around modte hain. Mandrel ka size sariye ke thickness (diameter - d) pe depend karta hai.

Select Bar Type for IS 2502 Rule:

Bar Diameter < 25mm: 2d
Bar Diameter ≥ 25mm: 3d
Live Visualization
2d

Pin (Mandrel) radius directly affects the bending curve. TMT bars are brittle, so they need a larger pin to avoid cracking.

03. Standard Hooks (Anchorage)

Bar ko concrete me phasa ke rakhne ke liye hooks banaye jate hain. IS 2502 me 2 main type ke hooks aur bends defined hain.

Standard 180° Hook

Anchorage Value = 16d

  • 👉 180 degree ka ghumav.
  • 👉 Straight portion hook ke baad kam se kam 4d lamba hona chahiye (minimum 65mm).

Standard 90° Bend

Anchorage Value = 8d

  • 👉 90 degree ka ghumav.
  • 👉 Straight portion hook ke baad kam se kam 4d lamba hona chahiye.
  • 👉 Zyada tar column se footing junction ya slab edges me use hota hai.

04. Site Tolerances (Allowed Errors)

Site pe bar bender robot nahi hai. Millimeter to millimeter accuracy namumkin hai. IS 2502 allow karta hai kuch minor mistakes jise Tolerance kehte hain.

Condition / Type of Cut Length Constraint Tolerance Allowed (IS 2502)
Cutting Length of Straight Bar All lengths +25 mm / -25 mm
Length of Bent Bar Up to 1000 mm (1 meter) +10 mm / -10 mm
Length of Bent Bar 1000 mm to 2000 mm +15 mm / -15 mm
Length of Bent Bar Over 2000 mm +25 mm / -25 mm
