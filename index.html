2. Deep Technical Definitions

Mandrel (Pin)

Jis roller ya pin ke around sariya moda jata hai. IS code kehta hai sharp 90 degree nahi modna chahiye warna bar toot sakti hai. Mandrel ka internal radius bar ke diameter (d) par nirbhar karta hai.

Bend Deduction (Elongation)

Jab steel bend hota hai, outer curve stretch hota hai (tension) aur inner curve compress. Is wajah se bar ki total lambaai badh jati hai. BBS banate waqt is extra length ko minus karna zaroori hai.

Anchorage Value

Ek sariya concrete mein se khichkar bahar na aa jaye (bond slip na ho), iske liye usko hooks ya bends diya jata hai. Har bend ki ek anchorage value hoti hai. Standard U-hook ki value 16d tak hoti hai.

Centre Line Measurement

IS 2502 Clause 3.1.2 aur Table III ke anusaar, sariye ki bending dimensions hamesha uski Centre Line ke along measure ki jati hain. Outer-to-outer measure karne par formula badal jata hai.

4. Bends & Hooks Forming End Anchorages (Clause 3.2.1)

Standard 180° U-Hook (Fig. 1A)

Allowance (H) = Approx 9d
  • Ek standard U-Hook sariye ko concrete me lock karta hai.
  • k value: Mandrel ka radius. Mild steel ke liye k=2. TMT ke liye usually k=4.
  • 4d Rule: Curve khatam hone ke baad jo straight part bachta hai, wo kam se kam 4d lamba hona chahiye. (Aur minimum 65mm hona hi chahiye).

Standard 90° L-Bend (Fig. 1B)

Bend Deduction = 2d
  • Yeh bend columns se footing ya slab edges par istemal hota hai.
  • Formula Logic: Jab sariya 90 degree ghumta hai, toh total lambaai me roughly 2d ka izafa (elongation) hota hai. Isliye hum lambaai se 2d minus karte hain.
  • Isme bhi bend ke baad straight hissa kam se kam 4d hona chahiye.

5. Binders, Stirrups, Links (Clause 3.8 & Table V)

Stirrups (Rings) columns aur beams ko shear force se bachate hain aur main bars ko apni jagah par pakad kar rakhte hain. Isme 135-degree ke hooks banaye jate hain.

The Crucial "8d / 10d" Rule

IS 2502 (Clause 3.8) specifically kehta hai: "In the case of binders, stirrups, links, etc, the straight portion beyond end of curve at the end shall be not less than eight times (8d) the nominal size of the bar."

Note: Earthquake prone areas (seismic zones) mein modern codes (jaise IS 13920) ise badha kar 10d ya 75mm (whichever is greater) kar dete hain.

Cutting Length Formula (Rectangular Ring)

2 × (A + B) + (2 × Hook Length) - (Bend Deductions)
  • A & B = Inner core dimensions (Column Size - 2x Cover).
  • Hook Length = Generally taken as 10d or 12d per hook for 135° bends.
  • Bend Deductions = 3 corners of 90° (3 × 2d) + 2 corners of 135° (2 × 3d).
